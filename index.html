Astronomers have unveiled the first image of the supermassive black hole at the centre of our own Milky Way galaxy.
This result provides overwhelming evidence that the object is indeed a black hole and yields valuable clues about
the workings of such giants, which are thought to reside at the centre of most galaxies.
The image was produced by a global research team called the Event Horizon Telescope (EHT) Collaboration,
using observations from a worldwide network of radio telescopes.
